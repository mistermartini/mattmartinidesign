Making Content

(9/26)

As the first article for my website, I feel some pressure to make worthwhile content, to write something meaningful, with impact. “Write something that shows the world what a badass graphic designer I am!”. But therein lies the problem. I'm not interested in making this website as a way to show myself as more than what I already am, or making something that is beyond my current skillset in HTML and CSS. I have high hopes for this site, but basically, I want to use it as a custom showcase for writing and visual work that I do.

It may not always look like an amazing portfolio, and it may branch out into a wide variety of things, but I want it to grow with me and be a unique display of what I do, in a clean, simple style.
